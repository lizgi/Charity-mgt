About Our Charity

The local charity Organizations are among the most adversely affected groups by the Covid 19 Pandemic. Majority of the donors are losing their ability to support these organizations because of the economic impact left behind by the pandemic. Furthermore, finding new donors is also an enormous challenge, leaving the thousands of beneficiaries impoverished. Charity is an act of kindness where someone donates what they have to the less fortunate.

Our aim is to come up with a platform where we link both the donors and the Ngo's that need help. Having a website that one can see and access from time to time will solve both the ngo and the donor from alot of hustle. On the donor's side they get to see the approved Ngo's request from different categories,vet to see if they really deserve the funding,.

This platform will be beneficial on the NGO's side too,in that they have a better chance to be known and funded,. Having there online presence will grant them better chances of being funded by the many donors. Due to the heavy digital migration of alot of firms and businesses it's only recommendable that this two fronts also get to be seen and heard. This online will atracts alot of people from just ordinary people to moneyed people hence it will have a huge net of funds,hence the NGO's can remain afloat.
